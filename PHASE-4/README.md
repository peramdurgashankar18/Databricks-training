## 🔄 ETL Workflow

```text
customers.csv
      │
      ▼
 Data Cleaning
      │
sales.csv
      │
      ▼
   Join Tables
      │
      ▼
Business Transformations
 ├── Daily Sales
 ├── City Revenue
 ├── Repeat Customers
 ├── Customer Segmentation
      │
      ▼
Final Reporting Table
      │
      ▼
Export CSV Report
```

## 📈 Business Tasks Completed

### ✅ Task 1 – Daily Sales

Calculated the total sales generated for each day.

**Output**

- Order Date
- Total Sales

---

### ✅ Task 2 – City-wise Revenue

Calculated the total revenue generated by customers from each city.

**Output**

- City
- Total Revenue

---

### ✅ Task 3 – Top 5 Customers

Identified the top five customers based on total spending.

**Output**

- Customer Name
- Total Spend

---

### ✅ Task 4 – Repeat Customers

Retrieved customers who placed more than one order.

**Output**

- Customer ID
- Order Count

---

### ✅ Task 5 – Customer Segmentation

Classified customers based on their total spending.

| Segment | Condition |
|----------|-----------|
| 🥇 Gold | Total Spend > 10000 |
| 🥈 Silver | 5000 – 10000 |
| 🥉 Bronze | Less than 5000 |

---

### ✅ Task 6 – Final Reporting Table

Generated a consolidated business report containing:

- Customer Name
- City
- Total Spend
- Order Count
- Customer Segment

---

### ✅ Task 7 – Export Report

Exported the final reporting table as a CSV file.

```python
final_df.write \
    .mode("overwrite") \
    .option("header","true") \
    .csv("/samples/output/report")
```
